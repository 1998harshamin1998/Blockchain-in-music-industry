How It Works?

Producers start by registering on our platform. They create contracts of their albums and decide the per view royalty. Registered Distributors on our platform can then view those contracts and accept them as per their own choice. A request is generated for Producers. Upon accepting, contracts are then set as Active contracts. At the end of each cycle a new transaction block is added to the blockchain with transaction details as number of views or access to the albums made by people on distributors platform. Distributors then distribute the royalty to the producers as per contract.

STEP 1: Registeration

Producers and Distributors register on our platform.

STEP 2: Create Contract

Producers create new contracts of their albums and songs.

STEP 3: Confirmation Dialog

Distributors can accept those contracts upon which they are shown in producers confirmation dialog.

STEP 4: Active Contracts

When a contract is accepted by both producer and distributor, they are set as active contracts. Transaction logs with details such as number of access/views to the album are then stored in blocks of blockchain. This is done in transaction cycles.

STEP 5: Revenue Distribution

Fair revenue is given to the producer by the distributor as per contract terms. Revenue is generated automatically using details of the transaction block.
